Biography

Chidimma Chisom Umeogu is a geospatial analyst and environmentalist with various experiences in Geographic Information Science, Remote Sensing, and atmospheric/environmental sciences ranging from application of Geospatial techniques in Vegetation Sensitivity to Environmental factors, Land Use and Land Cover Changes analysis, Surface Water mapping, Fecal Sludge Management, Biodiversity Conservation Monitoring, Urban Heat Island mapping, Land Degradation analysis and application of GIS in Utility Industry for customer and assets enumeration amongst others.
Currently carrying out a doctoral research on SENSING THE FOREST PULSE FROM SPACE: VEGETATION PHENOLOGY MONITORING IN THE PRESENT CLIMATE, supervised by Prof. Christian I. Enete
